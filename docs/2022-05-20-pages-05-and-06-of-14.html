St. Elizabeth’s Medical Center offers patients access to some of the finest orthopedic surgeons, musculoskeletal physicians and rehabilitation doctors in Boston. Our team specializes in innovative treatment options for orthopedic injuries including sports injuries, fractures, spine issues, hand injuries, shoulder surgery, foot and ankle, women’s orthopedic health, concussion management and more.

Renowned, Board-Certified Boston Orthopedists Excel at Caring for Patients with Joint Pain and Muscle Problems

Our highly skilled team of orthopedic surgeons, physician assistants, nurses, rheumatologists, athletic trainers, and physical therapists work together to ensure every patient receives individualized treatment plans and the highest quality of care available.

Rehabilitation can be an important part of a patient’s recovery after an orthopedic injury. Our orthopedic team works with every patient to ensure they receive the appropriate ongoing support in their rehabilitation. St. Elizabeth’s Medical Center has its own Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation Program located on our Brighton campus as well as new Boston Sports Performance Center in Wellesley, MA.
