Emergency Care

Our safe and ready Emergency Department combines world class medical expertise with first-in-class safety protocols so you’ll get the right care, treatment, and attention you need – right here in our hospital.

St. Elizabeth’s Emergency Department, staffed by board-certified emergency physicians and specially trained nursing and support staff, cares for more than 30,000 patients a year.

St. Elizabeth’s 24-Hour Emergency Room

St Elizabeth’s offers patients some of Boston’s most advanced imaging, including the city’s only 256-slice CT scanner to provide more accurate diagnosis of a variety of medical ailments.

Services Provided by St. Elizabeth’s Emergency Department

  • Adult, OB-GYN, pediatric and psychiatric emergency medical services
  • In-house consultants for all medical and surgical specialties
  • Immediate access to critical hospital services such as diagnostics and imaging, allowing for expedited evaluation and treatment
  • Coordinated care throughout the spectrum of medical specialties resulting in comprehensive medical care
  • Patient-centered care that honors privacy and the patient and family’s right to participate in care
  • Expedited triage, bedside registration, and 32 treatment rooms to reduce wait time
  • 24-hour access to emergency cardiac catheterization and the Stroke Center
