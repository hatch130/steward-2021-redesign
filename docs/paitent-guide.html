St. Elizabeth’s Medical Center Hotel and Lodging Guide

St. Elizabeth’s welcomes you and your family to our medical center. It is important to note that hotel and inn rates can change and are also seasonal. When calling hotels, please confirm the rate after mentioning that you are a patient at St. Elizabeth’s Medical Center. You may also need to provide documentation of your care at the hospital in order to obtain the discounted rate.
